Classic Omelette Recipe

Quick, easy, and satisfying. This traditional omelette brings together eggs cooked just right, with an option to add your favorite cheese, veggies, or meats.

Time Required

  • Total: Around 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients List

  • 2-3 fresh eggs
  • Salt, to your liking
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional extras: shredded cheese, diced vegetables, cooked ham or bacon

Instructions

  1. Whisk the eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and beat until well blended. For a fluffier texture, add a splash of milk or water.
  2. Heat the skillet: Place a non-stick pan on medium heat, then melt the butter or add oil.
  3. Pour in the eggs: Once the butter is foamy, pour the eggs in. Tilt the pan to spread them evenly.
  4. Optional fillings: When the eggs are nearly set, scatter your desired fillings over half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and finish: Gently fold the omelette in half over the fillings. Let cook for an additional minute, then serve.
  6. Serve and enjoy: Transfer to a plate and garnish with extra salt and pepper if needed.
